// how we can work together

Collaboration Opportunities

Various ways companies, organizations, and individuals can partner with UCC.

Event Sponsorship

Sponsor our CTF competitions or annual events. Get brand visibility among Malaysia's growing cybersecurity student community and connect with top talent.

CTF Collaboration

Organize a CTF competition together or invite our team to participate. We can also help design challenges and provide judging expertise.

Workshops & Talks

Send your security experts to speak at UiTM, or invite us to host workshops at your organization. We specialize in practical, hands-on security training.

Internship & Hiring

Access our pool of skilled cybersecurity students for internship programs and graduate hiring. Our members are battle-tested through real competitions.

Media Coverage

We can help spread your news and stories to our large community of cybersecurity enthusiasts!

Research Partnership

Academic and industry researchers interested in cybersecurity education, student performance in CTFs, or security tooling can collaborate with our team.
