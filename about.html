// our story

The Cyberheroes Origin

UiTM Cyberheroes Club (UCC) was born from a shared passion among a few students who wanted to hack, break and defend real systems.

Starting with a small group of dedicated individuals, UCC has grown into one of UiTM's most active technical clubs, competing in international CTF competitions and fostering a culture of continuous learning and knowledge sharing.

Today, we stand as a bridge between academia and industry that produce UiTM graduates who are not just degree holders, but battle-tested security professionals.

  • Participate in local & international CTF competitions
  • Host workshops and training sessions for all skill levels
  • Collaborate with industry partners and government agencies
  • Publish technical writeups and security research
  • Mentor new members through structured learning paths
// our purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To train a generation of skilled cybersecurity professionals through competitive CTF training, knowledge sharing and real-world security challenges in UiTM Shah Alam.

Our Vision

To be Malaysia's most impactful university cybersecurity club and recognized nationally and internationally for excellence in security competitions, research and talent development.

Our Values

Integrity, curiosity, collaboration and continuous improvement. We believe in ethical hacking, open knowledge, and lifting each other up.

// what we offer

Activities & Programs

From beginner talks to advanced CTF workshop, we have something for every level of cybersecurity enthusiast.

CTF Training & Competitions

Monthly meetup to covers training for all categories. We compete in local events like HackMalaysia, Wargames.MY, and international competitions on platforms like CTFtime.

Technical Workshops

Hands-on workshops and CTF chall on web exploitation, pwn, crypto, forensics, OSINT and more! Tailored to current industry needs.

Guest Lectures & Talks

We regularly invite industry professionals, security researchers and CISO-level experts to share insights on career paths, current threats and cutting-edge security research.

Certifications & Career Support

We guide members toward industry certifications (CEH, OSCP, CompTIA Security+) and provide career mentorship, resume reviews, and internship connections with our industry partners.
