// what we do

Built for Cyber Heroes

We train, compete and grow together - turning students into security professionals through hands-on experience.

CTF Competitions

We actively participate in local and international Capture The Flag competitions, solving challenges across web, pwn, crypto, forensics, and reverse engineering.

Workshops & Training

Regular internal workshops on penetration testing, malware analysis, network security, and more - led by experienced members and guest speakers.

Community Building

A welcoming community for all skill levels. From beginners learning their first exploit to veterans chasing top rankings on global platforms.

Research & Writeups

We document our challenge solutions and security research, sharing knowledge with the community through detailed technical writeups.

Competitions & Awards

Representing UiTM on the national stage - consistently placing in the top ranks of Malaysian cybersecurity competitions and events.

Industry Collaboration

Partnering with companies, agencies,- and researchers for internships, mentorship programs, and collaborative security projects.
